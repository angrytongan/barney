A quick hack combining GreenSock's ScrollTrigger, Apache ECharts, drag-drop and Papa Parse. This demonstrates the editor function of dropping a previously prepared .csv file, and the reader experience of scrolling the data.

Right click and save one / some / all of these files (data from Our World In Data)

  • Daily Covid Vaccine doses
  • Daily Covid tests
  • Life expectancy

then drag and drop them in the area below. Do the scrolly thing. You can drop files that you generate yourself. 3 columns, first column is series name, 2nd is the date, third is a value. Have a look at the supplied files above for more detail.
